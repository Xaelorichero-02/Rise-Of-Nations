Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Rise of Nations

Why Are Some Start Dates "Empty"?

We are working on one era at a time in chronological order. A mod of this size doesn't just appear out of thin air; it takes hundreds of hours to create. Keep in mind we are not just replacing Mussolini with a cat; we are taking a game that's 12 years long and expanding it to 160 years long.

Why Are There Mods in the Credits List That Haven't Been Used?

The mods in the credit list are mods that gave us permission back when we were Totaler Krieg. Although we plan on redoing everything ourselves this time around, we keep them on our credit list to honor their generosity and good spirit, which is what modding is all about.

Why Are the Rules So Strict on Our Discord?

We don't like trolls. Simple as that.

OMG, Rise of Nations Is the Name of a Game!

Fair Use

OMG, You Stole Stuff From Other Mods!

The whole purpose of modding is sharing. If we do decide to use something from another mod, it will only be with their express permission, backed by a screenshot. We are not competing with any other mods, as we are a timeline expansion mod.

How Do I Change to the Next Era Focus Trees?

It is done automatically for you. There is nothing more to say...

I Can't Change Such and Such Law!

If you can't change a law, you either do not meet the requirements, or the law changes automatically by event.

What Scenarios Do You Recommend?

You can't go wrong with 1900 or 1933...

What Are the Different Eras?

  • Dawn of a New Century (DNC): 1900–1909 (9 Years)
  • World War 1 (WW1): 1910–1918 (8 Years)
  • Interwar (INT): 1919–1932 (13 Years)
  • World War 2 (WW2): 1933–1948 (15 Years)
  • Early Cold War (ECW): 1949–1979 (30 Years)
  • Late Cold War (LCW): 1980–1999 (19 Years)
  • Modern Day (MD): 2000–2030 (30 Years)
  • Era of Tomorrow (EOT): 2030–2060 (30 Years) (not implemented yet)

Will You Add an Economy/Trade System?

Probably yes.

I Can't Load the Mod, It Keeps Crashing!

Some people get this issue. While we do not know what is causing it, we can offer you a solution:

  • Roll back to 1.15.4

The Mod Doesn't Load, It Loads Vanilla Instead!

If the mod launches vanilla, make sure that it is inside the playset you are currently using. If it still doesn't work, clear your user folder. If that still doesn't work, unsubscribe from the mod, close your game and launcher, wait a few minutes, then resubscribe.

How Can I Join the Dev Team?

You simply apply here... But please think twice before applying!

Custom Focus Tree for X Country When?

We are going to do an event, decision, and focus tree expansion for each era, one at a time.

What Are You Working on Right Now?

Will You Make GFX Updates?

GFX changes are not standalone updates; we are improving icons and portraits in every update we release. Should you find any bad portrait, please report it in our Discord #bad-portraits channel.

I Asked for Content, but You Are Not Working on It!

We are working on what the community wants; we are doing polls to ask what the next update should be. Make sure to participate in the polls.

When Will There Be a Translation for [Insert Language]?

Due to the massive amount of work to be done to bring this project to completion, any translation that gets done will be done by the community or a specially dedicated team. We have enough on our hands.
