Join Our Development Team

Rise of Nations is looking for talented and dedicated individuals to join our volunteer development team. If you're passionate about history, game design, or modding and want to contribute to one of the largest HOI4 total conversion mods, we'd love to hear from you!

Available Positions

Mod Developers

Create and implement gameplay mechanics, events, decisions and focus trees using the HOI4 scripting language.

2D Artists

Design focus icons, event pictures, portraits, UI elements and promotional graphics.

Mappers

Create and edit provinces, states, strategic regions and map textures for historical accuracy.

Writers & Researchers

Research historical events and figures, write event text, national focuses and leader biographies.

Music Composers

Create original music tracks and soundtracks fitting different eras and nations in the mod.

Community Managers

Engage with our community, moderate our Discord and help organize multiplayer events.

Application Form

Application Submitted!

Thank you for your interest in joining our team. We will review your application and contact you via Discord if we feel you'd be a good fit.

Important Notes:

  • This is a volunteer project. All positions are unpaid.
  • We prioritize applicants who can demonstrate their skills and commitment.
  • You must be a member of our Discord server for us to contact you.
  • Most communication happens through Discord, so having access is essential.
