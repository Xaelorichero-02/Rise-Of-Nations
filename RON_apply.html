Read Before Applying

Requirements

1) DECENT LEVEL OF ENGLISH.
If you can't read and write in English we won't be able to communicate properly.
2) AVAILABILITY
Only consider joining if you are able to give us at least 1 hour a day. We all have real life stuff to do.
3) BE 18 YEARS OLD OR OLDER
This rule is mostly because so far most 14 year olds who have joined, have absolutely slacked off or really just want a role, without doing the work. If you are younger than 18, but willing to work hard and help out, then you are welcome to join us.
4) WILLINGNESS TO LEARN
Are you willing to learn? If so then we can teach you.
5) THE RIGHT SOFTWARE
- GitHub Desktop to Push your Changes to the Mod
- GIMP Photo Editor or Equivalent to Edit GFX
- Visual Studio Code or Equivalent to Edit the Mods Files

Application

1) WHAT ARE YOUR SKILLS? (GIMP, PHOTOSHOP, FOCUS TREES, EVENTS, FLAGS, PORTRAITS, ETC)
2) WHY DO YOU WANT TO JOIN RISE OF NATIONS?
3) DO YOU FEEL COMFORTABLE SPEAKING IN ENGLISH?
4) ARE YOU 18 OR ABOVE? IF NOT, ARE YOU A RESPONSIBLE PERSON?
5) HOW MANY HOURS A WEEK CAN YOU DEVOTE TO THE MOD? BE REALISTIC & HONEST.
6) DO YOU KNOW THAT MODDING CAN BE TEDIOUS/BORING?
7) DO YOU HAVE ANY PREVIOUS MODDING EXPERIENCE? IF SO PLEASE ATTACH LINKS TO BACK IT UP. IF YOU DON’T, ARE YOU WILLING TO LEARN?
