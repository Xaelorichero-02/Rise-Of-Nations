Rise of Nations: Final Strike 1.6.0 (HOI4 version: 1.15.0)

Additions

- Added Special Projects to the mod, including vanilla and new ones
- Added inner circle for WW2 Germany
- Added BBA states for Ethiopia
- Added vanilla Landmarks models to the map
- Added Propaganda campaigns for most majors during WW2, which gives different bonuses depending on the campaigns choosen
- Added a starting GUI to choose the gameplay option, with optimisation and gameplay settings
- Added GUI and mapmode to make formable nations easier to form
- Added GUI and mapmode for Economic sphere mechanic
- Added custom achievements to RoN
- Added an economic system
- Added Modern Day USA content (Focus tree, events and decisions)
- Added A Focus tree for Communist China for the cold war era

Bugfixes

- New Mechanics for the cold war period
- Fixed a lot of errors and crash issues
- Fixed plane issues not having range assigned, or non modifiable once designed (for BBA users)
