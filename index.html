Explore a New Era of Grand Strategy

Rise of Nations expands the Hearts of Iron IV experience with revolutionary features and historical depth

Extended Timeline

Experience over a century of history from 1900 through both World Wars, the Cold War, and into the modern day

Historical Depth

Dozens of unique focus trees for major and minor nations with immersive historical events and decisions

Enhanced Mechanics

Completely reworked economy, war exhaustion and laws

Alternative History

German WW1 victory scenario, German WW2 victory scenario, Zombie mode and more
